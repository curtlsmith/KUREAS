Saphire FTL Format

Click "Load Example" to see a sample fault tree, or create your own file:

TIMELINESS, ALARM =
ALARM          OR  ALARM01 ALARM02 ALM-MECH
ALARM01         OR  ALM-FTS ALM-SWT
ALARM02         AND ALM-BPF ALM-CPF
^EOS
TIMELINESS, PERSONAL =
PERSONAL         OR  OTHER SICK SICK-FAM
^EOS

Format: Each section begins with "TREE_NAME, GATE_NAME =" followed by gate definitions. Gates are defined as "GATE_NAME OR/AND INPUT1 INPUT2 ...". Sections end with ^EOS.
